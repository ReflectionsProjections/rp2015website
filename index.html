WHAT IS REFLECTION PROJECTIONS?

Reflections | Projections is a tech conference organized and run by students at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. We draw students, speakers, and companies from across the US and the world!

WHEN IS REFLECTION PROJECTIONS?

The conference will take place from October 1st to 4th in 2015 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

WHAT IS MECHMANIA?

Mechmania is an artificial intelligence programming competition that takes place over 24 hours during the conference. Teams compete to write the best AI to complete a new game every year, with the best team earning not only a prize, but bragging rights for the year, and a prestigious place in the annals of RP history.

WHAT IS PUZZLEBANG?

PuzzleBang! is a cryptography competition that takes place over the course of the event. Solve puzzles, decrypt hidden messages, and follow the trail of clues to receive a secret reward!
